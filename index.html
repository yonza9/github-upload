About Us

oneTimeShop was formed by a group called, Laizard. The purpose of creating this website is not only to find a retail shop in a shopping mall but the users actually can use this website to do some analytic. The shopping mall consists of three floor, each floors have four to five shop that are available.

The users can try to choose a shop, and make some analytic such as sort it according to the popularity or sort it according to age group.

Shopping Mall

This section allows you to view the surrounding of the shopping mall. There are some features and functions that u can used such as view the shopping mall with specific angle, or view the floor with specific angle.

Floor Panel

This section allows you to view the floor of the shopping mall. The shopping mall consists of three floor, ground floor is restaurant, 1st floor is fashion and 2nd floor is electronics gadget.
